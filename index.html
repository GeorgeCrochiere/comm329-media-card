Minesweeper App

A project made for the purposes of the Clarkson University Open House. This project consists of the creation of a minsweeper app with adjustable parameters that would allow users to play the game, while providing options to play new rounds after the game finished.

For this project, I was the sole developer, utilizing what I had been learning in my Android App Development class.
